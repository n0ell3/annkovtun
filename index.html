Ann Kovtun is an alt-pop artist, producer, sonic world-builder, and songwriter. Her music is a Library of States — a universe where each song acts as a "book" exploring a specific emotional state through fictional characters. Her work sits at the intersection of immersive storytelling, vocal performance, psychology, and minimalist electronic production.

Following the breakout of the track Паніка (Panic), her English-language album EMERALD DEW, produced by Ann in Norway, received a Main Banner on Apple Music Ukraine and broad editorial support. Since then, she has been expanding the Library through a series of singles that introduce listeners to characters from the darkest depths of the psyche.

Beyond her solo releases, Ann's artistry extends to international theatre, film, and video games, including featured vocal work on the Annie Award-nominated Teslagrad 2.

By blending textural electronics with elements of voice acting and fantasy, Ann Kovtun transforms Ukrainian authenticity into global introspective themes.

Her music serves as a soundtrack for moments experienced by fictional characters and real people alike — creating a safe space to Feel the Unseen.
